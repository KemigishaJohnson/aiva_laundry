About AIVA Laundry and Detergent services.

Our Mission

AIVA Laundry and Detergent Services is a professional company dedicated to providing convenient, reliable, and efficient laundry solutions. Established in 2025, we use state-of-the-art equipment and eco-friendly practices to deliver exceptional results.

Our Services

  • Laundry Services: Wash, dry, fold, and iron
  • Detergent Supply: Wholesale and retail

Our Commitment

We prioritize customer satisfaction, ensuring timely and efficient services tailored to individuals and businesses (hotels, hospitals, schools). AIVA is your trusted partner for all laundry needs.
